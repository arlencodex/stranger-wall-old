How We Review

Every report is read by a real person, not an automated filter. We check the exact message you quoted, assess whether it violates the community terms, and remove it if it does — usually within 24 hours.

Because Stranger Wall is built on anonymity, we cannot notify you of the outcome. We also cannot tell you who posted the message — we simply don't know. The removal happens quietly and permanently.
