Contact us

We are committed to responding to every enquiry with care and clarity. Whether your matter is technical, legal, or personal — we will address it directly.

Stranger Wall operates without a traditional support infrastructure by design. All enquiries are handled personally by our core team, ensuring that each message receives genuine attention rather than an automated response.

We aim to respond to all correspondence within 24 hours. For content removal requests, please provide the exact text of the message in question along with its approximate posting time in UTC — without IP logging, this is the only reliable method for us to locate and action specific content.

Response & Resolution

All submissions are reviewed directly by a member of the Stranger Wall team. If a reply email was provided, you will receive a personal response. Where no email is given, any necessary action will be taken silently and without public acknowledgment — consistent with the platform's principles.

Content removal requests are actioned within 24 hours of receipt, provided the exact message text is supplied. Privacy and legal matters are handled within 72 hours with a formal written response.

Stranger Wall is an independent platform. There is no corporate layer between your message and the people responsible for this project. Every enquiry is treated with the seriousness and discretion it deserves.
